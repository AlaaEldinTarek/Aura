Privacy Policy

App: Aura | هالة  ·  Package: com.aura.hala  ·  Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Alaa Eldin Tarek ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Aura | هالة mobile application ("the App"). By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Account information
When you create an account we collect your email address and, if you sign in with Google, your Google account name and profile picture. Guest mode collects no account information.

b) Location
The App requests your device location only to calculate accurate Islamic prayer times and Qibla direction for your area. Location data is processed on-device and is never stored on our servers. You may also enter your city manually without granting location permission.

c) User-generated content
Tasks, prayer tracking records, Dhikr counters, Quran bookmarks, and Wird progress that you create are stored in your account on Firebase Firestore so they sync across your devices.

d) Usage analytics
We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated data about how features are used (e.g. which screens are visited). This data cannot identify you personally and is used solely to improve the App.

e) Crash reports
If the App crashes, anonymous diagnostic information may be sent automatically to help us fix bugs.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Camera, microphone, or contacts
  • Precise location stored on our servers
  • Advertising identifiers or third-party tracking
  • Payment or financial data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalise the App's core features (prayer times, tasks, Quran)
  • Sync your data across devices when you are signed in
  • Send local notifications for prayer times, task reminders, and Wird goals — these are scheduled entirely on your device
  • Analyse anonymous usage trends to improve the App

4. Permissions Explained

  • Location – calculates prayer times and Qibla direction.
  • Notifications – delivers prayer alerts and task reminders.
  • Exact Alarm / Use Exact Alarm – ensures prayer notifications fire at the correct second.
  • Foreground Service – runs a minimal countdown service so the next prayer time is always visible in the notification shade.
  • System Alert Window – displays the Focus Mode overlay above other apps during a timed study/work session.
  • Modify Audio Settings – silences the device ringer automatically during prayer time (optional feature, off by default).
  • Ignore Battery Optimisations – prevents the OS from killing prayer alarms when the device is in deep sleep.
  • Read Media Audio – reads custom Adhan audio files you have downloaded inside the App.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) – authentication, cloud database (Firestore), file storage, and analytics. Firebase Privacy
  • Google Sign-In – optional sign-in method. Google Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile → Delete Account option inside the App. Upon deletion, all Firestore data linked to your account is permanently removed within 30 days.

Local data (SharedPreferences, downloaded Adhan files, cached Quran pages) is removed when you uninstall the App.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Firebase Firestore enforces authentication-based security rules so only you can access your data. No passwords are stored by us — authentication is handled entirely by Firebase.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

١. المعلومات التي نجمعها

أ) معلومات الحساب

ب) الموقع الجغرافي

د) إحصاءات الاستخدام

  • بيانات الدفع أو المعلومات المالية

٣. كيف نستخدم معلوماتك
